第一段【写作技巧】

Innovation**(创新)** is very important.

  1. be of importance.

    • Innovation is great importance.

  2. play an important role.

    • Innovation plays an extremely important role.

  3. nothing is more important than..

    • Nothing is more important than innovation.
    • Nothing can play a more important role than innovation.
